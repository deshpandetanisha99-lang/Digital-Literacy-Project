PREVENTION CHECKLIST

GENERAL ONLINE SAFETY
1) Use strong, unique passwords for each account(mix of letters, numbers, symbols) Avoid using your name or birthdate.
2) Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, banking, and social media.
3) Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments, even if they appear to come from friends.
4) Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
5) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities (banking, logging into important accounts).


SOCIAL MEDIA AND PRIVACY
1) Set your profiles to private and limit who can see your posts and personal information.
2) Do not overshare details like phone number, address, college ID, or daily routine.
3) Think before posting—digital footprints are permanent and can impact your future opportunities.


FINANCIAL SAFETY
1) Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
2) Always verify payment requests before approving—remember: receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN.
3) Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, especially from strangers or untrusted sources.
4) Use only official apps like BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, and ensure they are updated.

AWARENESS AND RESPONSE
1) Be cautious of job scams, scholarship frauds, and fake internships asking for upfront fees.
2) If you are harassed or bullied online, do not engage—block and report immediately.
3) Save evidence (screenshots, messages) in case you need to report the incident.


REPORTING CYBERCRIME
1) Report incidents at: National Cyber Crime Portal- https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
2) Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
