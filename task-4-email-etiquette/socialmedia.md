DO's
Think before posting– Consider how your post may be perceived by others.
Maintain privacy settings– Regularly check and update your account privacy.
Be respectful and professional– Communicate politely, even in disagreements.
Verify information before sharing– Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.
Build a positive digital footprint– Share content that reflects your skills, interests, and values.


DON'Ts
Don’t overshare personal information– Avoid posting sensitive details like address, phone number, or financial info.
Don’t engage in online harassment or trolling– It can harm others and your own reputation.
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content– This can affect academic and career opportunities.
Don’t fall for scams or suspicious links– Be cautious of unknown messages or offers.
Don’t use social media irresponsibly during academic hours– It can impact productivity and focus.
